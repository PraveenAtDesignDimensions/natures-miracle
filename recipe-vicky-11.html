SWEET PEPPER FRITTER MlRACLE WITH STRAWBERRY TOMATO CHUTNEY & OLIVE POWDER

INGREDIENTS FOR THE FlLLING
  • 6-8 Natures Miracle Sweet Peppers
  • 250g cooked potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp jeera seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seed powder
  • 1/2 tsp asafoetida
  • 1/2 tsp mango powder
  • Salt n' pepper
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
FOR THE BATTER
  • 1 cup gram flour
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/3 tsp ajwain
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Oil
FOR THE CHUTNEY
  • Heat oil, add curry leaves, Natures Miracle Tomatoes and Strawberries , add seasonings and cook for 5 minutes. Add balsamic vinegar and orange zest
  • Cook until dry
  • Let it cool & finish with fresh herbs.
INGREDIENTS FOR STRAWBERRY TOMATO CHUTNEY
  • 8-9 Natures Miracle Strawberries
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/3 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/3 tsp nigella seeds
  • 1/3 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp crushed black pepper
  • Curry leaves
  • 1/3 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Sea salt
  • 30 ml orange juice
  • Orange zest
  • 1/2 cup peeled and c opped Natures Miracle Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Tarragon
  • Coriander
  • Parsley
METHOD
  • For stuffing, make a pocket-styled slit in the Sweet Snack Peppers.
  • Heat oil, add some cumin seeds to crackle.
  • Add cooked mashed potato and mix in all the spices. Stir for 5 minutes.
  • Add seasoning and set it aside.
  • Use the mixture to stuff Natures Miracle Peppers.
  • Whisk gram flour, corn starch, baking soda, baking powder, a pinch of turmeric, red chilli and cumin powder to make a smooth batter.
  • Let the batter rest for 30 minutes.
  • Dip the Natures Miracle Peppers in the batter generously, then deep fry it in oil of your choice.
