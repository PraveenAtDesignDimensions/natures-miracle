When we started thinking about premium food stores to tie up with, Le Marche was the obvious choice. Since Le Marche was already established as a premium food store brand for the past decade, launching Nature’s Miracle at the store gave us instant visibility, customer base as well as credibility.


Le Marche is already selling 'the Good Food Story' and our produce shares its mandate to provide healthy, fresh, nutritious food and snack options. Our packaging is carefully designed to maintain freshness and educate the customer about nutritive content without hampering product visibility or portability.
