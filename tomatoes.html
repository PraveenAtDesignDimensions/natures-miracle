From snackable little munchies to king size!

Fresh greenhouse tomatoes are extremely versatile and can be utilized in a wide array of dishes, from conventional to experimental. Eat them fresh from the pack, grilled, mixed, puréed or whichever way you want because they add delight in every bite.

Nutritional value and health benefits
  • High in lycopene, Vitamin A and C, antioxidants and fibre.
  • Cholesterol free.
  • Tomatoes reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases and blood related disorders.

Sweet Like Candies

Pop-in Ready

Cherry on Vine

Enriched Salad Companion

Packed with Natural Freshness

Your Delicious Curry’s Best friend

Enjoy a cherry that turned tomato but couldn’t leave the vine. We bring not only the fruit but the vine to your table for you to pluck and have them fresh.

These should be called ‘tomato soup on the vine.’ Cook for 15 minutes and you will have the finest tomato soup you have ever tasted. But no, you won't stop there. You will want to try these red vine tomatoes in sauces, jams, and salads – baked, grilled or raw. The possibilities are endless!
