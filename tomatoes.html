Tomatoes

From snackable little munchies to king size!

Fresh greenhouse tomatoes are extremely versatile and can be utilized in a wide array of dishes, from conventional to experimental. Ideal for grilling, roasting, sautéing, or as a perfect on-the-go snack!


Eat them fresh from the pack, grilled, mixed, puréed or whichever way you want because they add delight in every bite. Packed with vitamins and nutrients for all your cooking, these tomatoes excite your imagination and spoil your taste buds. Bite into their succulence for your daily health fix or throw a feast at your table-they are suited to every requirement.

Nutritional value and health benefits
  • High in lycopene, vitamin A and C, antioxidants and fibre. Cholesterol free. It reduces the risk of cardiovascular diseases and blood related disorders.

Candy Tomatoes

Sweet Like Candies

Packed with Vitamins and Minerals

Pop-in Ready

Our succulent candy tomatoes are a treat for kids and adults alike. Enjoy candy like sweetness packed with nutritious benefits. Enjoy the, taste of nature at her best anytime, anywhere.

Stew them. Paste them. Sauce them. Use them to thicken other sauces and soups. Get adventurous! Oven-roasting or drying will intensify the taste, making a punchy tomato pesto or excellent bruschetta topping.

Candy tomatoes are hand planted & picked to ensure maximum quality and flavour. Bursts of sweetness with the perfect amount of crunch, this tomato is ideal for snacking.

Snack Cherry Tomatoes

Sweet & Sour

Juicy and Tasty

Packed with Lycopene

You will be wowed every time you pop in a cherry tomato! With no chemical residue; our beautiful, firm and nutritious cherry tomatoes are perfect for tossed salads, pastas or just snacking away those hunger pangs between meals.

The snack cherry tomato is an impressively versatile tomato – eat raw, cooked, grilled, sauced, dried, in salads or as a snack. Makes an ideal lunch companion! A salad and cooking mainstay, Nature’s Miracle snack cherry tomatoes are layered little delights. Savor the flavour that takes you through a subtle sweet, tangy, to candy burst. Fall in love with the healthy way of life.

Cherry on Vine

Enriched Salad Companion

Packed with Natural Freshness

Your Delicious Curry’s Best friend

Enjoy a cherry that turned tomato but couldn’t leave the vine. We bring not only the fruit but the vine to your table for you to pluck and have them fresh. These pollution-protected cherry vines are grown in our glass encased greenhouse to give you the best of health.

These should be called ‘tomato soup on the vine.’ Cook for 15 minutes and you will have the finest tomato soup you have ever tasted. But no, you won't stop there. You will want to try these red vine tomatoes in sauces, jams, and salads – baked, grilled or raw. The possibilities are endless!

King Tomatoes

Packed with lycopene

Your salad and dressing companion

Juicy and Tasty with Roughage

Revel in the textbook perfection of our king tomatoes. Get these big and luscious plump tomatoes to enhance the texture and flavour of your gravies, salsas, burger patties, sauces, juices, soups, stuffed sandwiches etc. Dice them or mash them, these king tomatoes act as a perfect foundation for any dish.

This garden-fresh beauty is the ultimate salsa tomato and will surprise you on the grill as a thick-sliced patty substitute. King tomatoes are the ideal crushing tomato with lots of juice and make a lovely base for cold sauces and dips.
