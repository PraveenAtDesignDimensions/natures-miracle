Hydration that goes ‘crunch’

Amazing for raw snacking!

Crunch is the new proof!

Enjoy with green skin

You can now literally eat your water with our cucumbers. Made of 90% water, these miniature wonders ensure that you stay hydrated while beating the hunger blues. You do not have to worry about small children carrying water bottles if they learn to snack on these. A delightful source of vitamins and fibre, our snack cucumbers are equally enjoyable when consumed straight from the pack or tossed in salads. Now crunch your way to health with cucumbers, which have zero bitterness. An ideal healthy snack for children and for busy people on the go.

Nutritional and Health Benefits
  • No bitterness and No peeling required for eating Snack Cucumbers.
  • A crunchy and crisp vegetable that is a good source of Vitamin K and fibre.
  • Fresh cucumbers are known to have antioxidants, anti-inflammatory and anti-cancer properties.
  • Cucumbers are a good source of phytonutrients and are low in calories.
Usage as raw snack
  • Just 3 snack cucumbers already make up half the daily requirement of vegetables.
  • It is a great choice for hunger pangs in between meals and can be enjoyed anywhere, anytime of the day.
