Sweet and colourful gourmet experience

Nature’s Miracle offers sweet bell peppers in a variety of colours. Packed with a sweet to tangy flavour with the crunch of power packed freshness, our peppers are sure to delight. Re-discover premium snacking with these binge-able peppers. A colourful source of vitamins in their vibrant reds, yellows and oranges, making each dish you conjure up an artistic masterpiece. Truly the perfect snack or addition to any meal!

Nutrition and health benefits:
  • Bell peppers are very high in vitamin C. They also are a good source of vitamin K1, vitamin E, vitamin A, folate, and potassium.
  • Bell peppers contain many healthy antioxidants, including capsanthin, violaxanthin, lutein, quercetin, and luteolin. These plant compounds are associated with many health benefits.
  • Lutein and zeaxanthin, for example, are said to improve eye health when consumed in adequate amounts.
Usage as an ingredient:
  • Can be consumed fresh and raw as an on- the go snack.
  • Toss some chunks of peppers in a salad with some delicious sauces.
  • Bell peppers can be stuffed, sautéed, barbequed or baked for a delicious meal.
